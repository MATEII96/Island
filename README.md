Hello!

One day I thought about how the 1 million pixels page owner made lots of money so easily.
I knew I had to try something similar.

So I decided to make a 30x30 grid with islands that you can buy with real money, put names and then put accessories: house, palm tree, coral, campfire, fish and others, everything with coins that you buy with real money.

Every day you can like 3 islands and the owner of the island you like gets 1 coin.

I split the grid into 3 parts:

- the border (most affordable part)
- the middle (more pricey but not most expensive)
- the center (most expensive part)

The idea is to make people communicate differently and leave a fingerprint in the history of the internet if some people didn't have the opportunity to leave a fingerprint about 15 years ago.
